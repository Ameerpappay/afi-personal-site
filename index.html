About Afifa amassery

Afifa amassery is a clinical psychologist with over 3 years of experience in the field. She specializes in cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) and mindfulness-based approaches to treat anxiety, depression, and stress-related disorders. Her goal is to create a safe and supportive environment where clients can explore their challenges and develop effective coping strategies.

Our Services

We offer specialized therapy services to meet your unique needs. Explore our offerings to find the right support for you and your loved ones.

Couple Therapy

Our couple therapy sessions provide a safe and neutral space for partners to navigate challenges, improve communication, and strengthen their relationship. We work collaboratively to address conflicts and build a more resilient and loving partnership.

Child Therapy

We offer compassionate and effective therapy for children facing emotional and behavioral challenges. Our play-based and age-appropriate approaches help children express themselves, develop coping skills, and thrive in their environment.
